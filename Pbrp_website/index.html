Who We Are

Please be rude productions is a film studio based in Huntsville, Texas created by Presley Eveland, a college student studying film/cinematography. Currently, the studio only creates shortfilms, but has ambitions to further move onto larger productions.

They operate with mostly the same crew, being classmates within the same major, helping eachother get productions complete. Please be rude productions produces mostly horror based films, showcasing their skill for practical effects and makeup design.
