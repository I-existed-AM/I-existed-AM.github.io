Director

Presley Eveland is a writer/director based in Huntsville, Texas. She created and organized the "Please be rude productions" brand when she started her film making studies at SHSU after her high school gradustion. She now works at a movie theatre, Cinemark, where she watches, and also plans her next films and ideas.

Our Story

Please be rude productions started out as a concept between college students, and with the direction of Presley Eveland, became what it is today. we worked long hours, and crammed as much as we could into the night. And it all paid off when we got a theatrical release of our film "Backstabber". We continue to work on bigger productions, hoping to be a well known brand in the film making industry.
